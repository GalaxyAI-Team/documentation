You can view your full credit usage history — including a per-tool breakdown — at [magica.com/usage](https://magica.com/usage). The dashboard shows which features are consuming the most credits, so you can make informed decisions about your usage.

## Checking your usage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the usage dashboard">
    Navigate to [magica.com/usage](https://magica.com/usage). Log in if prompted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your total credits used">
    The top of the page shows your overall credit consumption for the current billing period alongside your current balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Drill into the per-tool breakdown">
    Scroll down to see a breakdown by tool or feature. This lets you identify which actions consume the most credits.
  </Step>
</Steps>

In addition to the dashboard, a toast notification appears every time you use a feature, showing the credits consumed by that specific generation.

## Why you may be prompted to buy credits with a large balance

Even if your credit balance appears large, some advanced generations require more credits for a single run than your current available balance. High-end video, large-scale image models, and other compute-intensive tasks can have per-generation costs that exceed your balance — this is not a lockout, just an insufficient balance for that specific run.

When this happens you have two options:

- Purchase additional credits to cover the generation
- Switch to a lighter model or lower quality setting that costs fewer credits per run

<Note>
  Your monthly credits refresh on the same date each month. If you are close to the limit, you can wait for the renewal or top up with an add-on credit package. Add-on credits never expire.
</Note>

## Headshot generator credit costs

The headshot generator and AI travel photo generator require training a custom LORA model, which consumes a significant number of credits. This is a one-time cost per model. Once the model is trained, generating additional headshots, travel photos, or using it in Photo Studio costs minimal credits.

Other tools on Magica do not have this upfront training cost.

## Resolving usage discrepancies

If the usage stats look incorrect:

1. Log out of your account and log back in to refresh the session data.
2. Contact support at [magica.com/support](https://magica.com/support) if the numbers still appear wrong after re-logging in.

<Tip>
  To verify credits refunded from failed generations, visit [magica.com/usage?show=credited](https://magica.com/usage?show=credited). Refunds from complete failures are processed within 24–48 business hours.
</Tip>
