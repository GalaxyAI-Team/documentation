Magica does not offer refunds due to the upfront cost of reserving AI supercomputing resources. This policy is outlined in the [Refund Policy](https://magica.com/refund-policy) at sign-up.

**Detailed Explanation**

* Your subscription payment secures the AI computing power from upstream providers, similar to a non-refundable ticket.
* You can cancel anytime to avoid future charges.

**Troubleshooting & Tips**

* If you have been incorrectly charged or encountered a billing error, reach out to support.
* For issues about subscription cancellations, see “How to cancel my subscription?”
