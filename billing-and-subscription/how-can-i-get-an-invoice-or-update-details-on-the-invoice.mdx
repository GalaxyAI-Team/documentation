#### 💻 If you subscribed via the website:

Visit [https://go.magica.com/invoice](https://go.magica.com/invoice) and enter the **email you used for payment**.

You’ll be able to:
* View/download your invoices
* Edit invoice details like company name, tax ID, and address

#### 📱 If you subscribed via the App Store or Play Store:

For detailed receipts and invoices from App Store purchases, check your [Apple Account subscriptions](https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions).

For Google Play purchases, check your [Google Play subscriptions](https://play.google.com/store/account/subscriptions).
