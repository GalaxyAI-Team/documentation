All payments on Magica are processed through Stripe, which accepts all major credit and debit cards. PayPal is not currently supported. For mobile subscribers, billing is managed by Apple or Google directly.

## Getting your invoice

How you access your invoice depends on where you subscribed.

### Website subscribers

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the invoice portal">
    Visit [go.magica.com/invoice](https://go.magica.com/invoice).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your payment email">
    Enter the email address you used when subscribing. This may differ from your Magica login email if you used a different address at checkout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View or download your invoice">
    Your invoices are listed. You can download them as PDFs or edit details such as your company name, tax ID, and billing address directly in the portal.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Mobile app subscribers

If you subscribed through the iOS App Store or Google Play, Magica does not have access to your billing information. Contact Apple or Google directly to retrieve invoices for mobile purchases.

## Payment declined by Stripe

Magica does not control Stripe's payment decisions. If your payment is declined, try subscribing through the Magica mobile app instead:

- **iOS:** [go.magica.com/iOS](https://go.magica.com/iOS)
- **Android:** [go.magica.com/android](https://go.magica.com/android)

Once subscribed via the mobile app, sign in with the same account on the web — your subscription and credits sync automatically across platforms.

## Subscription not showing after payment

If you completed payment but your subscription or credits are not appearing, the most common cause is a mismatch between the email used for payment and the email on your Magica account.

To resolve this:

1. Search your inbox for an email from **humans@mail.magica.com** — this email contains your correct plan details and the email address on file.
2. Sign out of Magica and log back in using that exact email address.
3. Avoid using Apple Privacy Relay addresses (`@privaterelay.appleid.com`) — these can prevent the system from detecting your subscription.

<Note>
  If you signed up with an Apple Privacy Relay address, sign out and create or log in to a standard email-based account, then contact support to transfer your subscription.
</Note>

If the issue persists after following these steps, contact support at [magica.com/support](https://magica.com/support) with your payment confirmation.
