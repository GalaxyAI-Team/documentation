You can cancel your Galaxy.ai subscription at any time. The steps depend on where you originally subscribed — through the Galaxy.ai website, the iOS App Store, or the Google Play Store.

<Warning>
  Download or save any valuable media **before** you cancel. Canceling permanently deletes your chat history, media generations, and saved prompts — this data cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

## Cancel via the Galaxy.ai website

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into Galaxy.ai">
    Go to [galaxy.ai/sign-in](https://galaxy.ai/sign-in) and sign in to your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Manage Billing">
    Open the left sidebar and click **Manage Billing**. This opens the Stripe billing portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel or switch your plan">
    In the Stripe portal, select the option to cancel your subscription. You can also switch to a different plan from this screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you cannot log in to your Galaxy.ai account, go directly to the Stripe billing portal at [billing.stripe.com/p/login/4gw2bP5Qmdn0bA](https://billing.stripe.com/p/login/4gw2bP5Qmdn0bA) to manage your subscription without signing in.
</Note>

## Cancel via iOS App Store or Google Play

If you subscribed through Apple or Google, you must cancel through their subscription management — not through Galaxy.ai. Galaxy.ai does not have access to mobile subscription billing.

- **iOS:** Open **Settings** → tap your name → **Subscriptions** → find Galaxy.ai → **Cancel Subscription**
- **Android:** Open the **Play Store** → tap your profile icon → **Payments & subscriptions** → **Subscriptions** → find Galaxy.ai → **Cancel**

## What happens after you cancel

- Your chat history, media generations, and saved prompts are permanently deleted
- You lose any special lifetime discount rate you had locked in — if you resubscribe, you pay the then-current regular pricing
- Previous data and media cannot be recovered

If you were on a promotional price, canceling means you permanently lose that discounted rate. Re-subscribing in the future will be at the regular price in effect at that time.

## Refund policy

Galaxy.ai does not offer refunds. Your subscription payment secures AI computing power from upstream providers, similar to a non-refundable ticket. You can cancel at any time to stop future charges.

If you believe you were incorrectly charged or encountered a billing error, contact support at [galaxy.ai/support](https://galaxy.ai/support).

## Unauthorized charges

If you see a charge you do not recognize, first check whether anyone else with access to your payment method may have signed up. To stop future charges, follow the cancellation steps above. You can also reach support directly at [galaxy.ai/support](https://galaxy.ai/support).

## Card charged twice

Stripe automatically flags duplicate charges. If your card was charged more than once for the same subscription, expect a refund from Stripe within 3–5 business days. Contact support if you do not receive it within that window.
