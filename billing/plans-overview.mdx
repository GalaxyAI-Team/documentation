Magica offers four plan types to fit different budgets and team sizes. All plans give you access to the same AI tools; the main differences are billing frequency, credit allocation, and whether the plan is for an individual or a group. Visit [magica.com/pricing](https://magica.com/pricing) to see current pricing and subscribe.

<Note>
  Magica is not related to Samsung Galaxy phones. It is a separate AI platform that requires its own subscription.
</Note>

### Lifetime plan details

The Lifetime plan provides 15 million credits per month for life — this is **not unlimited**. Credits renew automatically every month, and you can purchase additional credits at any time if you need more in a given month.

Upgrading from an annual plan to Lifetime cancels your annual plan immediately and activates Lifetime access. The remaining period of your annual subscription is not refunded.

Lifetime subscriptions are for **individual use only**. If you need access for multiple users, use a Team Plan instead.

## Team plans

Team plans are designed for organizations with 5 or more members. They include:

- Admin dashboard with usage stats per member
- Centralized billing
- Team collaboration features

| Billing | Price |
|---|---|
| Monthly | $15 per user / month |
| Annual | $96 per user / year (save ~47%) |

Minimum of 5 seats required. Get started at [magica.com/create-organization](https://magica.com/create-organization). For enterprise needs, contact [enterprise@magica.com](mailto:enterprise@magica.com).

### Non-profit and educational discount

Eligible non-profits and educational institutions can receive 50% off team plans (minimum 5 members). Contact [sales@magica.com](mailto:sales@magica.com) with proof of status to apply. This discount does not apply to Lifetime plans.

## Choosing between individual and team

| | Individual | Team |
|---|---|---|
| Users | 1 | 5+ |
| Tool access | Full | Full |
| Admin dashboard | — | Yes |
| Centralized billing | — | Yes |
| Collaboration features | — | Yes |

If you are a solo user, any individual plan gives you full access to all tools. If you need to manage multiple users with shared billing and usage visibility, choose a Team Plan.

<Info>
  If you sign up during a promotional period, your discounted rate is locked in for as long as you maintain your active subscription without cancellation. Canceling forfeits the promotional rate permanently.
</Info>
