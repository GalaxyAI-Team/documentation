Workspace Settings is where you configure how Magica behaves across your sessions — from your default AI model to custom context that helps the AI understand you better.

## Accessing workspace settings

Click the gear icon beside **Recent** in the left sidebar to open Workspace Settings.

## Setting a default AI model

In Workspace Settings, select your preferred AI model — for example, GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, or Gemini. Magica saves your selection and loads that model automatically on your next visit.

<Note>
  If the model reverts to a different option, your browser may be blocking cookies or local storage. Clear your browser cache and try again.
</Note>

## Custom instructions

Custom Instructions let you give the AI persistent context about yourself so it can tailor its responses without you repeating the same information every session.

To add custom instructions:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace Settings">
    Click the gear icon beside **Recent** in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find the Custom Instructions field">
    Scroll to the **Custom Instructions** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Type any context you want the AI to always keep in mind — your role, communication style, preferred output format, or anything else relevant.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Save your changes. The instructions apply to all future conversations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prompt library and quick-insert

Save your most-used prompts at [magica.com/saved-prompts](https://magica.com/saved-prompts). Once a prompt is saved, type `/` in any supported text field to open the quick-insert menu and select it.

<Tip>
  If a saved prompt contains variables, a dialog will appear so you can fill in the values before inserting.
</Tip>

## Web search

Magica can search the internet in real time to give you up-to-date answers. Click the globe icon in the message input bar to enable web search for your current conversation.

<Note>
  If you're not seeing web-based results, check that the globe icon is toggled on.
</Note>

## Feature requests

Have an idea for Magica? Submit it at [magica.com/feature-requests](https://magica.com/feature-requests). Highly-voted requests get prioritized on the product roadmap.

## Unsubscribe from emails

To stop receiving Magica newsletter emails, scroll to the bottom of any Magica email and click the unsubscribe link.
