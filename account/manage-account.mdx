---
title: "Manage your Magica account"
description: "Update your email, recover your password, switch to dark mode, log out, and delete your Magica account — essential account tasks in one place."
---

Your Magica account controls access to every tool, subscription, and saved content on the platform. This section covers the most common account tasks, from changing your email to permanently deleting your account.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Change your email address">
    Magica support can update your email address. Open a support ticket at [magica.com/support](https://magica.com/support) and include both your current email address and the new one you want to use.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Recover your password">
    Magica uses magic codes — you don't need a password to sign in. Enter your email on the login page and Magica will send you a one-time code.

    Once you're signed in, you can set or update a password:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open your profile">
        Click your profile icon in the bottom-left sidebar.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Go to Security">
        Select **Security** from the menu.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Update your password">
        Enter and save your new password.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    You can also go directly to [magica.com/forgot-password](https://magica.com/forgot-password) to reset via email.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Log out">
    Click your profile picture or avatar in the bottom-left sidebar, then select **Sign Out**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Switch to dark mode">
    Go to **Utilities** in the bottom-left sidebar, select **Theme**, then choose **Light**, **Dark**, or **System** to match your device preference.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Delete your account">
    Visit [magica.com/delete-account](https://magica.com/delete-account) to permanently delete your account.

    <Warning>
      Account deletion is permanent. It removes all your chat history, media generations, and saved prompts with no recovery option.
    </Warning>

    <Warning>
      If your company team account uses the same email as your personal account, deleting your personal account will revoke **all** access tied to that email — including your company team access. Secure your company team access independently before deleting any personal account.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Your account shows as banned">
    If you sign in and see a "banned" message, this typically means your account has been deleted — either by you or via a deletion request. This is not a traditional ban; it's how Magica indicates the account no longer exists.

    There is no unban or recovery option. Sign up again using a different email address to continue using Magica.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
