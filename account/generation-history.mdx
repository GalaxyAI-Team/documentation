---
title: "View and manage your generation history"
description: "Access past AI-generated images, videos, and audio from each tool's history page, and download your media before the 30-day storage limit."
---

Magica keeps a history of everything you generate so you can revisit, download, or build on past results. History is organized by tool rather than in a single centralized feed.

## How to access your history

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the tool you used">
    Navigate to the AI tool you want to review — for example, the AI Image Generator or AI Video Generator.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click View History">
    Each tool page has a **View History** button. Click it to see all previous generations for that specific tool.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find and download your content">
    Browse your past results and download anything you want to keep.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  There is currently no centralized history page across all tools. Go to each tool individually to see its unique history.
</Note>

## Storage duration

Media — including images, videos, and audio — is stored for **30 days**, then automatically removed from Magica's servers.

<Warning>
  Download any generated media you want to keep within the 30-day window. There is no way to recover files after they are deleted.
</Warning>

## Troubleshooting

If you don't see past creations in a tool's history, make sure you're signed into the same account you used when you generated them. Generations are tied to the account that created them and won't appear under a different login.
