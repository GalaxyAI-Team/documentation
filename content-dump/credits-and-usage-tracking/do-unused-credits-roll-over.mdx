---
title: "Do unused credits roll over?"
category: "Credits & Usage Tracking"
crispSlug: "do-unused-credits-roll-over"
---

Monthly subscription credits roll over and never expire. Any unused credits will accumulate in your account, ready for you to use anytime.

* If you buy an extra credit package (beyond the monthly included credits), those will remain in your account until used.
* Your monthly credits do **not** expire—they continue rolling over, so you can use them whenever you need.

### Troubleshooting & Tips

* If you find you need more credits some months, consider purchasing an add-on pack—they never expire.
* Track your usage in the [Magica Usage Dashboard](https://magica.com/usage).
