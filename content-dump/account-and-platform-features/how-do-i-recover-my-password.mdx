If you are already logged in, you can change your password from Account settings:

[https://magica.com/?settings=account](https://magica.com/?settings=account)

If you cannot log in, use the forgot password page to reset access:

[https://magica.com/forgot-password](https://magica.com/forgot-password)

Helpful links:

* Sign in: [https://magica.com/sign-in](https://magica.com/sign-in)
* Change password while logged in: [https://magica.com/?settings=account](https://magica.com/?settings=account)
* Forgot password: [https://magica.com/forgot-password](https://magica.com/forgot-password)
