#### 💻 If you subscribed via the website:

Visit [https://go.magica.com/invoice](https://go.magica.com/invoice) and enter the **email you used for payment**.

You’ll be able to:
* View/download your invoices
* Edit invoice details like company name, tax ID, and address

#### 📱 If you subscribed via the App Store or Play Store:

Unfortunately, we don’t have access to invoices or billing info for mobile subscriptions. Please check directly with Apple or Google for invoice support.
