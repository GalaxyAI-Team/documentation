Galaxy.ai is built around the principle that your data belongs to you. Everything you generate stays private, you retain full ownership, and Galaxy.ai never uses your content to train its AI models. This page explains how your data is handled and what controls you have.

## Your content is private

All user-generated media — text, images, videos — is private by default. Only you can access it. Galaxy.ai does not share your content publicly.

## Galaxy.ai does not train on your data

Galaxy.ai does not use your content or data to train its AI models. Training data is completely separate from the private user data processed during tasks. Your data remains private and secure.

## You own what you create

You have full control and ownership of all content you generate on Galaxy.ai. This includes **100% commercial use rights** — all designs and media generated via Galaxy.ai are free for commercial use with no licensing restrictions.

## Media storage

Generated media (images, videos, audio) is stored for **30 days**, then automatically removed from the system.

<Warning>
  Download any important creations before the 30-day window closes. Once removed, they cannot be recovered.
</Warning>

<Note>
  Always update your account privacy settings to align with your organizational policies.
</Note>

## Enterprise data security

If you have specific enterprise data security concerns, contact [security@galaxy.ai](mailto:security@galaxy.ai) directly.
