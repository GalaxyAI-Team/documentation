The Galaxy.ai affiliate program lets you earn commissions by referring new subscribers to the platform. Once you sign up and get approved, you receive a unique referral link and a unique promo code — two ways to make sure every referral gets credited to you.

## How to sign up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the affiliate sign-up page">
    Go to [galaxy.ai/refer](https://galaxy.ai/refer) and complete the sign-up form to apply for the affiliate program.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get approved">
    After your application is reviewed and approved, you receive access to your affiliate dashboard and your unique referral link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find your referral link and promo code">
    Return to [galaxy.ai/refer](https://galaxy.ai/refer) at any time to find both your referral link and your unique promo code. Share either — or both — to start earning.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your referral link and promo code

Every affiliate gets two attribution tools:

- **Referral link** — a unique URL that automatically tracks clicks and sign-ups from the visitors you send to Galaxy.ai.
- **Promo code** — a short code your referrals enter at [galaxy.ai/referral-code](https://galaxy.ai/referral-code). When redeemed, the new user gets **10 million bonus credits** and is mapped to your affiliate account — including any purchases they made in the past 30 days.

<Tip>
  Share both your referral link and promo code whenever possible. If link tracking fails due to an ad blocker or private browsing, your promo code still captures the referral.
</Tip>

## What purchases earn commissions

Commissions apply to purchases made on both the Galaxy.ai website and the mobile app.

<Note>
  Add-on credit purchases are **not** included in affiliate commissions. Commissions apply to all other purchases, including subscriptions.
</Note>
