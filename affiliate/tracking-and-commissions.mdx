Once you have referrals coming in, use the affiliate dashboard to monitor your performance and payouts. If a referral does not appear automatically, your promo code gives you a reliable fallback that works regardless of browser tracking settings.

## View your affiliate dashboard

Log in to your affiliate dashboard at [affiliates.magica.com/login](https://affiliates.magica.com/login). The dashboard shows:

- **Clicks** — the number of visitors who followed your referral link
- **Conversions** — referrals who subscribed and generated a commission
- **Payout status** — your pending and paid earnings

Commission rates vary by promotion and agreement. Check your dashboard or your affiliate agreement for the specific rate that applies to your account.

## Tips for maximizing attribution

Share **both** your referral link and your promo code every time you promote Magica. Using both together gives you the best coverage across different tracking environments:

- Your referral link captures most clicks automatically.
- Your promo code catches referrals where link tracking fails (ad blockers, private browsing, privacy extensions).

<Note>
  Promo codes can only be redeemed within **30 days** of the referred user's sign-up date.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting missing referrals

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why isn't a referral showing in my dashboard?">
    Tracking can fail when the referred user had privacy extensions, ad blockers, or private browsing enabled during sign-up. These tools prevent the tracking pixel or cookie from firing, so the click never registers.

    Share your promo code with the referral and ask them to redeem it at [magica.com/referral-code](https://magica.com/referral-code). This maps the referral to your account regardless of whether link tracking worked, and also includes any purchases they made within the past 30 days.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does my promo code do for the person I refer?">
    When a new user redeems your promo code at [magica.com/referral-code](https://magica.com/referral-code), they receive **10 million bonus credits** added to their account. It benefits both sides: they get extra credits, and you get the conversion credited to your affiliate account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does a referred user have to redeem my promo code?">
    Promo codes can only be redeemed within 30 days of the referred user's sign-up date. After 30 days, the code can no longer be applied to that account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My dashboard shows no data at all — what should I check?">
    Make sure your referral links are correctly formatted and pointing to Magica. A broken or modified link will not register clicks. Log out and back in to refresh your session, and confirm your affiliate account is in an approved state. If the issue persists, contact Magica support at [magica.com/support](https://magica.com/support).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
