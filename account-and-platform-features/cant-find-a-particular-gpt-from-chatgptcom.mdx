Third-party custom GPTs from OpenAI's marketplace aren't directly accessible inside Magica. However, you can create your own custom GPT assistants here: [chat.magica.com/assistants](https://chat.magica.com/assistants)

You can also submit a GPT link to **tools@magica.com** for evaluation.
