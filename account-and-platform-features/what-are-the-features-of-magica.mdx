Magica offers:

* Use premium AI services — All OpenAI models, DALL-E 3, Gemini, Claude, Perplexity, Flux, Stable Diffusion, Midjourney, Ideogram, ElevenLabs, HeyGen, and more
* Generate text, images, videos, music, speech, voice, and audio
* Create custom AI assistants (GPTs) trained on your data
* Chat with documents, articles, images, and videos
* Access 5,000+ AI tools, with new additions weekly
* No waiting period or hourly limits
* 100% commercial use rights included
* Your data is never used to train AI models
