---

If you're having trouble signing in to Galaxy.ai, the most common cause is a mismatch between the email you're using and the one your account was created with. Work through the steps below to restore access.

## Steps to try if you can't log in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh the page and try again">
    A temporary loading issue may prevent the login form from working. Refresh and attempt to sign in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use the exact email you signed up with">
    Galaxy.ai ties your account to a specific email address. Make sure you're entering the exact email used at sign-up.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox for a welcome email">
    Search your inbox for a message from **humans@mail.galaxy.ai**. This email contains your account details and confirms which address is registered.
  </Step>
  <Step title="On mobile, fully close and reopen the app">
    On iOS or Android, swipe the app away entirely and relaunch it before attempting to sign in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache and cookies">
    Stale cached data can interfere with the login flow. Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different browser or device">
    If the issue persists, open Galaxy.ai in a different browser or on a different device to rule out local environment problems.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Apple Sign-In and private relay emails

If you signed up on iPhone using **Sign in with Apple**, Apple may have generated a private relay email address (ending in `@privaterelay.appleid.com`) instead of using your real email. Use that private relay address to sign in — it is the address tied to your account.

<Note>
  Check your inbox for the welcome email from humans@mail.galaxy.ai to confirm which address Galaxy.ai has on file for you.
</Note>

## Password recovery

Galaxy.ai uses magic codes — you don't need a password to sign in. Enter your email on the login page and Galaxy.ai will send you a one-time code.

Once you're signed in, you can set or update a password:

1. Click your profile icon in the bottom-left corner.
2. Select **Security**.
3. Enter and save your new password.

You can also go directly to [galaxy.ai/forgot-password](https://galaxy.ai/forgot-password) to reset via email.

## "Banned" message when signing in

If you see a "banned" message, your account has been deleted — either by you or via a deletion request. This is not a traditional ban; it is how Galaxy.ai indicates the account no longer exists.

<Warning>
  There is no unban or recovery option for deleted accounts. Sign up again using a different email address to continue using Galaxy.ai.
</Warning>

## Subscription not showing after logging in

If your subscription or credits are not visible after you sign in:

1. Search your inbox for an email from **humans@mail.galaxy.ai** — this confirms your registered email address.
2. Sign out of your current session.
3. Sign back in using the exact email address from that email.

If you used an Apple private relay address (`@privaterelay.appleid.com`) at sign-up, sign in with that address. Using a different email creates a separate account with no subscription attached.
