Errors on Galaxy.ai can come from model providers, browser issues, or prompt problems. Most issues resolve quickly with a few targeted steps. This section covers the most common errors and how to fix them.

## General error steps

If you encounter an error, follow these steps in order:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Note the error message">
    Write down or screenshot the exact error text. This helps you search for a fix and speeds up support if you need to raise a ticket.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Refresh the page">
    A simple page refresh resolves many transient errors.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different model">
    Galaxy.ai gives you access to dozens of models. Switch to an alternative and try your generation again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your prompt">
    Review your input for anything the model may be rejecting — overly complex instructions, unsupported content, or formatting issues.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit a support ticket">
    If the issue persists, raise a ticket at [galaxy.ai/support](https://galaxy.ai/support) with your email, context about the error, and a screen recording if possible.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Support responses typically arrive within 48 hours.
</Info>

## Submit a bug report

Submit a screen recording of the issue at [go.galaxy.ai/bug-report](https://go.galaxy.ai/bug-report). Submitting a recording also makes you eligible for **15 million free credits** through the Free Credits Program.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Error code 422">
    A 422 error means the model provider is rejecting your inputs — this is not necessarily something you did wrong. Try tweaking your prompt or switching to a different model.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Output quality is low or not as expected">
    Galaxy.ai is an aggregator — outputs come directly from official model providers. Galaxy.ai does not control or modify the outputs.

    To improve results:
    - Write more specific, detailed prompts
    - Switch to a different model
    - Watch tutorials at [youtube.com/@LearnWithGalaxyAI](https://youtube.com/@LearnWithGalaxyAI)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Prompt Library is not accessible">
    The Prompt Library is part of the **Unfair Advantage** add-on and is not included in standard subscriptions. After purchasing the add-on, access the library at [galaxy.ai/prompts](https://galaxy.ai/prompts).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Music generator is not working">
    The music generator is currently under maintenance. Galaxy.ai will notify users once it is back online.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Chat with PDF is not working">
    Contact support at [galaxy.ai/support](https://galaxy.ai/support) and attach the PDF file you're trying to upload. The tech team will investigate directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Videos are not showing after generation">
    If your video does not appear immediately, visit [video.galaxy.ai/ai-video-generator](https://video.galaxy.ai/ai-video-generator) after 15–20 minutes. There is sometimes a slight processing delay before generations become visible.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
