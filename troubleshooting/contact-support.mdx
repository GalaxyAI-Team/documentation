Galaxy.ai support responds to most tickets within 48 hours. Use the right channel for your issue to get the fastest resolution. Include as much detail as possible — your account email, a description of the issue, and a screen recording if you can.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Support ticket" icon="ticket" href="https://galaxy.ai/support">
    The primary channel for all website-related issues. Go to [galaxy.ai/support](https://galaxy.ai/support) and raise a ticket with your email, context about the issue, and a screen recording if possible.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Mobile app support" icon="smartphone" href="mailto:app@galaxy.ai">

  </Card>

## Response time

Support typically responds within **48 hours**. For billing or account issues, always include the email address associated with your Galaxy.ai account in your message.

<Note>
  Web chat support is only available at [galaxy.ai/support](https://galaxy.ai/support) for website-related issues. For mobile app issues, use [app@galaxy.ai](mailto:app@galaxy.ai) — not the web support portal.
</Note>
