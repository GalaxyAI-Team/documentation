Magica support responds to most tickets within 48 hours. Use the right channel for your issue to get the fastest resolution. Include as much detail as possible — your account email, a description of the issue, and a screen recording if you can.

## Response time

Support typically responds within **48 hours**. For billing or account issues, always include the email address associated with your Magica account in your message.

<Note>
  Web chat support is only available at [magica.com/support](https://magica.com/support) for website-related issues. For mobile app issues, use [app@magica.com](mailto:app@magica.com) — not the web support portal.
</Note>
