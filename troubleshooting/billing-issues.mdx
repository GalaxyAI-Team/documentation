---
title: "Resolve billing and subscription issues"
description: "Fix missing Galaxy.ai credits, duplicate charges, declined payments, and unauthorized charges — and understand the no-refund policy and exceptions."
---

Most billing issues on Galaxy.ai stem from signing in with the wrong email, or from misunderstanding how the credit-based system works. The steps below cover the most common scenarios and how to resolve them.

## Subscription or credits not showing

This usually means you're signed in with a different email than the one used for payment.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find your registered email">
    Search your inbox for an email from **humans@mail.galaxy.ai**. This message confirms the email address tied to your subscription.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign out">
    Log out of your current Galaxy.ai session.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign back in with the correct email">
    Sign in using the exact email address found in the step above. Your subscription and credits should appear.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prompted to upgrade after paying

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log out and log back in">
    Sign out of Galaxy.ai and sign back in. This refreshes your account status and typically resolves the issue.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache and cookies">
    Clear your browser's cache and cookies, then try signing in again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try a different browser">
    If the issue persists, open Galaxy.ai in a different browser to rule out a local caching problem.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prompted to buy credits despite having a subscription

Galaxy.ai is credit-based. Even with an active subscription, if your current balance is insufficient for a specific generation, Galaxy.ai will prompt you to add credits. This is not a lockout — it means the generation you requested costs more than your available balance.

To continue, either purchase additional credits or wait until your credits refresh on your next monthly billing date.

## Card charged twice

Stripe automatically flags duplicate charges. You should receive a refund within **3–5 business days**. If you do not receive it by then, contact support at [galaxy.ai/support](https://galaxy.ai/support).

## Payment declined by Stripe

Galaxy.ai does not control Stripe's payment decisions. As a workaround, try subscribing through the Galaxy.ai mobile app:

- **iOS:** [go.galaxy.ai/iOS](https://go.galaxy.ai/iOS)
- **Android:** [go.galaxy.ai/android](https://go.galaxy.ai/android)

Once subscribed through the app, your account syncs and you can use Galaxy.ai on the web with the same login.

## Unauthorized charges

First, check whether someone else with access to your card may have signed up for Galaxy.ai.

To stop future charges, cancel your subscription:

1. Go to [galaxy.ai](https://galaxy.ai) and sign in.
2. Open the left sidebar and click **Manage Billing**.
3. Follow the prompts in the Stripe portal to cancel.

If you need further help, contact support at [galaxy.ai/support](https://galaxy.ai/support).

## Refund policy

<Warning>
  Galaxy.ai does not offer refunds. Your subscription payment secures AI computing resources from upstream providers upfront — similar to a non-refundable ticket. You can cancel anytime to stop future charges.
</Warning>

If you were **incorrectly charged** or encountered a billing error, contact support at [galaxy.ai/support](https://galaxy.ai/support). Billing errors are an exception to the no-refund policy.
