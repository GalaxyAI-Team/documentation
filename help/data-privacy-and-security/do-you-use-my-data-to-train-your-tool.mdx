No, Magica does not use your content or data to further train its AI models. Your data remains private and secure.

* You have full control and ownership of the content you generate.
* Training data is separate from the private user data processed during tasks.

**Troubleshooting & Tips**

* If you have specific enterprise data security concerns, reach out to security@magica.com.
