Every affiliate now has a **unique promo code** alongside their referral link at [magica.com/refer](https://magica.com/refer). Share this code — when redeemed at [magica.com/referral-code](https://magica.com/referral-code), the referral gets 10M bonus credits and is mapped to you (including past 30-day purchases).

**Note:** Promo codes can only be redeemed within 30 days of signup, and won't override existing affiliate mappings.
