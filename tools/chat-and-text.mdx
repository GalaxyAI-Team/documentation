## Features

### Web search

Enable real-time internet access for any model by clicking the **globe icon** in the message input bar. This lets the AI search the web and return up-to-date information, unlike models relying solely on their training data.

### Chat with documents

Upload PDFs, articles, images, and videos directly into the chat to ask questions about their contents. Supported file types include PDFs, images, and video files.

<Note>
  If Chat with PDF isn't working, contact support at [magica.com/support](https://magica.com/support) and share the file so the team can investigate.
</Note>

### Excel file processing

Upload an Excel file and Magica will process every row automatically, generating results for each item in your spreadsheet.

### Custom AI assistants

Create your own custom GPT assistants — trained on your instructions and context — at [chat.magica.com/assistants](https://chat.magica.com/assistants).

<Info>
  Third-party custom GPTs from OpenAI's marketplace aren't directly accessible inside Magica. You can submit a GPT link to [tools@magica.com](mailto:tools@magica.com) for evaluation, or build your own equivalent assistant at chat.magica.com/assistants.
</Info>

## Configuring your chat experience

### Set a default model

To make a specific model load every time you open the chat:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Workspace Settings">
    Click the **gear icon** beside "Recent" in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your preferred model">
    In **Workspace Settings**, select the model you want to use by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your selection">
    Your choice persists across sessions. If the selection reverts, check that your browser isn't blocking cookies or local storage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Custom Instructions

Give the AI standing context about you — your role, preferences, or working style — so every response is more relevant:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click the **gear icon** beside "Recent" in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Workspace Settings">
    Select **Workspace Settings** from the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your Custom Instructions">
    Type your context in the **Custom Instructions** field and save.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Custom Instructions are especially useful if you always want the AI to know your industry, preferred tone, or output format — you won't need to repeat it in every conversation.
</Tip>
