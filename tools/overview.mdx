Magica is an AI aggregator that brings together the world's best AI tools under one subscription. Instead of managing separate accounts for ChatGPT, Midjourney, Claude, and more, you get instant access to all of them — plus thousands of additional tools — in one place. Everything you generate is yours to keep, use commercially, and download at any time within 30 days.

## What's available

Magica organizes its tools into several categories:

- **Chat & Text AI** — Converse with GPT-5, Claude, Gemini, Grok, Perplexity, and many more models
- **Image Generation** — Create images with DALL-E 3, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, Flux, and Ideogram
- **Video Generation** — Produce AI videos, reels, talking photos, podcast videos, and UGC ads
- **AI Headshots** — Generate professional headshots trained on your own photos
- **Music & Audio** — Create AI-generated music, speech, and audio content
- **And more** — New tools are added every week across additional categories
