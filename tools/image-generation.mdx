- **Ideogram** — Excellent at embedding readable text in images

## Improving output quality

If your results aren't what you expected:

1. **Write a more specific, detailed prompt** — include style, lighting, composition, subject detail, and any negative constraints
2. **Switch to a different model** — each model has different strengths; what works poorly in one often works well in another
3. **Watch tutorials** — step-by-step guides are available at [youtube.com/@LearnWithMagica](https://www.youtube.com/@LearnWithMagica)

<Note>
  Magica is an aggregator — image outputs come directly from the official model providers. Partner providers do not offer refunds for generated content.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Error code 422">
    A 422 error means the model provider is rejecting your inputs — this isn't something you did wrong in terms of your account. Try tweaking your prompt (remove potentially sensitive terms, simplify unusual combinations) or switch to a different model.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Output looks low quality or unexpected">
    As an aggregator, Magica passes your prompt directly to the model provider and returns its output. If results are consistently poor for a given prompt, try refining the prompt with more descriptive language, or switch to a model better suited for that style of image.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I remove a watermark from an image?">
    Yes — use the **Object Remover** or **Text Remover** tool. Mark the area containing the watermark and the AI will remove it.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
