## Audio in videos

Most AI video models do not support spoken audio in the generated output — the audio track may come out as noise or gibberish.

<Warning>
  If you need spoken audio in your video, use **Veo3** or **Veo3 Fast** — these are currently the only models that support spoken audio. All other models are best used for silent or music-backed videos.
</Warning>

## How long video generation takes

Generation time varies depending on:

- **Video length** — longer videos take more time
- **Complexity** — detailed scenes with multiple elements take longer than simple ones
- **Model** — some models are faster than others
- **Provider load** — high demand on the model provider can add processing time

Explore all available video tools and current model options at [video.galaxy.ai](https://video.galaxy.ai).
