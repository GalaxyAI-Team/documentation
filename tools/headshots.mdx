The Magica AI Headshot Generator creates professional-quality headshots by training a custom AI model on photos you provide. Because the model learns your specific likeness, the results are far more accurate than generic AI portraits. You receive your headshots by email within 1–2 hours of submitting your training photos.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the AI Headshot Generator">
    Navigate to the AI Headshot Generator from your Magica dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload your training photos">
    Upload a set of clear photos of yourself. Use a variety of angles and lighting for the best results — avoid heavy filters or obstructions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your headshot styles">
    Select the styles, backgrounds, and looks you want the AI to generate for you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and wait for training">
    Submit your request. The AI trains a custom LORA model on your photos. This step consumes a significant number of credits because it requires dedicated model training.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your headshots by email">
    Results are typically delivered to your email within **1–2 hours**. You can also find them in your generation history.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your trained model for future use">
    After training completes, save your custom model. You can reuse it to generate additional headshots at a minimal credit cost — no need to re-train.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Credits and costs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why does the headshot generator use so many credits?">
    The AI Headshot Generator trains a custom LORA (Low-Rank Adaptation) model specifically on your photos. This personalized model training is computationally intensive and consumes significantly more credits than a standard generation. Once training is complete, generating additional headshots from the same model costs minimal credits.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I reuse my trained model?">
    Yes — and you should. After your model is trained, save it from your generation history. You can then generate new headshots in different styles or backgrounds without paying the training cost again.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use my model in Photo Studio?">
    Yes — your trained headshot model can also be used in Photo Studio to create other types of AI photos at low credit cost.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Generating headshots for multiple people

Currently, the AI can only train on one person at a time. To generate headshots for multiple people, train a separate model for each person and generate their headshots individually.

## AI travel photo generator

The AI travel photo generator works the same way — it trains a custom LORA model on your photos to place you in travel scenes. Expect a similar credit cost for the initial training, with low costs for subsequent generations.

<Tip>
  After your model is trained, save it immediately. Reusing a saved model to generate more headshots costs a fraction of the initial training credits.
</Tip>

<Note>
  Headshot results are delivered by email. Make sure the email address on your Magica account is correct before submitting your training job.
</Note>
