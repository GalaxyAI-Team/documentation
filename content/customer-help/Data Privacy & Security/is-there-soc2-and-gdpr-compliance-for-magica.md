title: Is there SOC2 and GDPR compliance for Magica?
-->
Yes, Magica is **SOC 2** and **GDPR** compliant, maintaining high standards for data security and privacy.

* SOC 2 compliance ensures robust data-handling procedures.
* GDPR compliance addresses how personal data is stored, processed, and transferred for EU users.

**Troubleshooting & Tips**

* For more detailed documentation, contact security@magica.com.
* Always update your account privacy settings to align with your organizational policies.
