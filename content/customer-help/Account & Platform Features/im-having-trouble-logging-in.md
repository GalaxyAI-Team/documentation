title: I'm having trouble logging in
-->
Try these steps:

1. Go directly to [https://magica.com/sign-in](https://magica.com/sign-in)
2. Refresh the page and try again
3. Use the exact email you signed up with
4. Check your inbox for a welcome email from humans@mail.magica.com
5. On mobile, fully close and reopen the app
6. Clear browser cache and cookies
7. Try a different browser or device

**Important:** If you signed up on iPhone, Apple may have used a private relay email (`@privaterelay.appleid.com`).

If you are already logged in and only need to change your password, use Account settings: [https://magica.com/?settings=account](https://magica.com/?settings=account).

If you cannot log in, reset access here: [https://magica.com/forgot-password](https://magica.com/forgot-password).

If you need a new account, use [https://magica.com/sign-up](https://magica.com/sign-up).
