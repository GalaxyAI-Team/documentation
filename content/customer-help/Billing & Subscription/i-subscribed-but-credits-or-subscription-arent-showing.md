title: I subscribed but credits or subscription aren't showing
-->
This usually happens when the account was created under a different email than the one used for payment.

1. Search your inbox for an email from **humans@mail.magica.com** — that includes your correct plan details
2. Sign out and log back in using that exact email
3. Avoid Apple Privacy Relay addresses (`@privaterelay.appleid.com`) — these can prevent the system from detecting your subscription
