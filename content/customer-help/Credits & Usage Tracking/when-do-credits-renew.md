<!--
title: When do credits renew?
-->
Your credits are added every month on the same date that you originally subscribed. For example, if you subscribed on March 15, your credits will renew on April 15, May 15, and so forth.

* Your billing cycle date stays consistent each month based on the original subscription date.
* You can find your next renewal date in your billing portal or through the dashboard.

**Troubleshooting & Tips**

* If your credits haven’t renewed as expected, try logging out and logging back in to refresh your account data.
* If renewal issues persist, please [raise a ticket](https://magica.com/support) with your subscription details.
