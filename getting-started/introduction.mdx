Galaxy.ai is an AI aggregator platform — think of it as Netflix for AI. Instead of paying for ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, DALL-E, and dozens of other tools separately, you get full access to all of them under one subscription. Every tool on the platform runs on the providers' official paid tiers, not free or throttled versions.

## What you can do with Galaxy.ai

With a single Galaxy.ai subscription you can:

- **Chat and write** — use ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and other leading language models
- **Generate images** — access DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, Flux, and Ideogram
- **Create videos** — produce reels, talking photos, and UGC ads with video generation tools
- **Make AI headshots** — generate professional profile photos from your own images
- **Compose music** — generate original tracks and audio with AI music tools
- And over 5,000 more tools across every AI category

## How it works

You pay one subscription fee. In return, Galaxy.ai gives you access to the paid versions of every AI provider it partners with — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, HeyGen, ElevenLabs, Runway, and more. You use credits to run generations; your credits refresh every month.

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Go to [galaxy.ai](https://galaxy.ai) and sign up with your email, Google account, or Apple ID.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a plan">
    Pick the plan that fits how you work — monthly, annual, lifetime, or team. See [Plans and pricing](/getting-started/plans-and-pricing) for a full comparison.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using tools">
    Open any tool from the dashboard and start generating. Your monthly credits are available immediately after subscribing.
  </Step>
</Steps>
