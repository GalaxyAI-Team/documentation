The Galaxy.ai mobile app gives you access to all the same AI tools available on the web — your account, credits, and generation history sync automatically across every device you use.

## Install and log in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the app">
    Open the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) and search for **Galaxy.ai**, or go directly to [go.galaxy.ai/iOS](https://go.galaxy.ai/iOS) or [go.galaxy.ai/android](https://go.galaxy.ai/android).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the app and tap Sign In">
    Launch the app after installation. Tap **Sign In** — do not create a new account if you already have one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with your existing credentials">
    Enter the same email and password you use on the web. If you signed up with Google or Apple, tap the corresponding button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using your tools">
    Your subscription, credits, and history load automatically. All generations you start on mobile are visible on the web, and vice versa.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Billing on mobile

<Note>
  If you subscribe through the iOS App Store or Google Play, billing is handled entirely by Apple or Google — not Galaxy.ai. To get an invoice for a mobile subscription, contact Apple or Google directly through your account settings.
</Note>

If your payment is declined on the website, subscribing through the mobile app is a reliable alternative. Once subscribed via the app, you can use your account on the web without any additional steps — everything syncs.

## Signing in with Apple

<Tip>
  If you signed up using **Sign in with Apple** and Apple assigned you a private relay email address (ending in `@privaterelay.appleid.com`), use that same private relay address when logging in on the web or Android. Using a different email creates a separate account and will not show your subscription.
</Tip>
