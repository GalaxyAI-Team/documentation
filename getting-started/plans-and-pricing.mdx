Galaxy.ai offers four plan types to fit individual creators, power users, and teams. All plans give you full access to every AI tool on the platform, commercial use rights, and automatic access to new models as they are added. See the full pricing page at [galaxy.ai/pricing](https://galaxy.ai/pricing).

## Individual plans

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Monthly — $15/month">
    The monthly plan is the best way to test the full Galaxy.ai experience before committing long-term. You get access to all tools with a monthly credit allowance. Cancel any time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Annual — save ~47%">
    Pay annually and lock in roughly half the monthly cost. Your credits and tool access are identical to the monthly plan — you just pay less per month.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Lifetime — one-time purchase">
    Pay once and keep your access forever. The lifetime plan includes **15 million credits per month**, refreshing automatically every month for life. It also includes access to all future AI models added to the platform.

    <Note>
      Lifetime means perpetual access with 15 million credits per month — not unlimited credits. If you need more in a given month, you can purchase additional credits separately.
    </Note>

    **Upgrading to lifetime** from an active annual plan cancels your annual subscription (no refund) and immediately upgrades your account. Lifetime upgrade benefits include:

    - VIP Priority Support with direct access to the technical team
    - 10x faster generation speed across all AI tools
    - Early access to new AI models before general release
    - Priority on feature requests
    - One-time bonus of 150 million credits

    To upgrade, visit [galaxy.ai/pricing](https://galaxy.ai/pricing) — the lifetime option is on the pricing page, not in account settings.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Team plans

Team plans are designed for groups of five or more people. They include all individual plan features plus collaboration tools and centralized account management.

| Billing | Price |
|---|---|
| Monthly | $15 per user/month (5-seat minimum) |
| Annual | $96 per user/year — save 47% |

**What team plans include:**

- Full access to all AI tools for every seat
- Admin dashboard with per-member usage statistics
- Centralized billing under one account
- Collaboration features across your team

To get started, visit [galaxy.ai/pricing](https://galaxy.ai/pricing). For enterprise needs, contact [enterprise@galaxy.ai](mailto:enterprise@galaxy.ai).

<Note>
  Lifetime subscriptions are for individual use only. If you need access for multiple people, use a team plan.
</Note>

## Non-profit and educational discount

Eligible non-profits and educational institutions receive **50% off team plans** (minimum 5 members). Contact [sales@galaxy.ai](mailto:sales@galaxy.ai) with proof of your organization's status to apply. This discount is not available on lifetime plans.

## Discount lock-in

If you subscribe during a promotional pricing period, your rate is locked in for as long as you keep your subscription active. Future price increases do not affect existing subscribers who secured the promotional rate.

<Warning>
  If you cancel your subscription, you permanently lose your locked-in promotional rate. If you resubscribe later, you pay the then-current regular pricing.
</Warning>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How is Galaxy.ai this affordable?">
    Galaxy.ai partners directly with AI providers — OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, and others — to bundle their paid versions under one roof. There is no catch: the goal is to make professional AI tools accessible without requiring separate subscriptions for each provider.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I try before committing to a longer plan?">
    Yes. Start with the monthly plan at $15 to experience the full platform. There is no free tier that provides access to all tools.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I always get the latest AI models?">
    Yes. All plans, including lifetime, include automatic access to new AI models as Galaxy.ai adds them from partner providers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
